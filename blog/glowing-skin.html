Real glow doesn't come from a serum. It doesn't come from a trending skincare product or a filter. It comes from what you nourish your body with — from the inside out. Your skin reflects your internal health, and when your body has what it needs, that radiance is visible and undeniable.

What is Collagen and Why Does Your Skin Need It?

Collagen is the most abundant protein in your body and the main structural component of your skin. It's what keeps your skin firm, plump, and smooth. The problem? Starting in your mid-20s, your body's natural collagen production begins to decline — by about 1–1.5% per year.

This means that by the time you notice fine lines, loss of firmness, or dull skin tone, your collagen levels may already have dropped significantly. Marine Collagen, especially in hydrolysed peptide form, is one of the most bioavailable and effective ways to replenish this.

📊
Collagen Benefits (backed by research): Studies show that consistent daily marine collagen supplementation for 8–12 weeks significantly improves skin elasticity, hydration, and reduces the appearance of fine lines. The effects are most notable in the face, neck, and hands.

Why Marine Collagen Over Other Types?

Not all collagen is equal. Marine Collagen (from fish) has peptide molecules up to 1.5x smaller than bovine (beef) collagen, which means it's absorbed into your bloodstream faster and more efficiently — making it the most bioavailable collagen source available. The Inside-Out Glow Routine

01

Take Marine Collagen daily — without skipping

Mix a scoop of Afrootic Marine Collagen into warm water, your morning tea, coffee, or smoothie. It's unflavoured and dissolves cleanly. Collagen works cumulatively — the more consistent you are, the better the results. Most customers notice visible changes in skin texture and nail strength within 3–4 weeks.

02

Pair with Vitamin C for maximum absorption

Vitamin C is a cofactor in collagen synthesis — meaning your body needs it to actually use the collagen you take. Squeeze lemon into your collagen water, have an orange with breakfast, or add a Vitamin C supplement. This combination significantly enhances the skin benefits of your healthy skin routine.

03

Stay consistently hydrated

Collagen works better when your body is well-hydrated. Water is skin's best friend — it maintains elasticity, flushes toxins, and keeps your complexion dewy and clear. Aim for 2–2.5 litres per day. Herbal teas and greens drinks count too.

04

Support gut health — it shows on your skin

The gut-skin axis is real. When your gut is inflamed or imbalanced, it often shows as breakouts, dullness, and redness. Adding Afrootic Greens and Probiotic Gummies to your routine creates a powerful inside-out beauty system — collagen firms from the outside, while the gut support clears and brightens from within.

05

Protect your collagen — reduce what destroys it

Certain habits actively break down collagen: excessive sugar consumption, chronic stress, smoking, alcohol, and unprotected UV exposure. While you work on building collagen in, also work on protecting it. Use SPF daily, manage stress through movement or breathwork, and reduce refined sugar where you can.
